Weekly Hybrid Workout Plan

4 Days/Week • 15–20 Minutes • Bodyweight + Minimal Equipment
Each workout = 3 rounds. Rest 30–60 seconds between exercises & rounds.

Pre-Workout Stretch Menu

Pick 3–4 movements to warm up before training. Flow intuitively or mix and match!

  • Jumping jacks x 30
  • Forward fold – 30 sec
  • Cat-cow x 10
  • High knees x 20
  • Neck rolls x 5 each way
  • Arm circles – 10 forward/backward
  • Hip circles – 10 reps
  • Lunging hip stretch – 30 sec/leg
  • Shoulder rolls – 10 reps
  • Inchworm to cobra – 5x
  • World’s greatest stretch – 2x/side
  • Standing side bends – 10/side
  • Ankle circles – 10 each
  • Torso twists – 20 reps

Day 1 – Full Body (Bodyweight)

  • Bodyweight squats – 20 reps
  • Incline push-ups – 10–15 reps
  • Glute bridges – 15–20 reps
  • Plank shoulder taps – 20 total
  • Wall sit – 30 seconds
  • Bird-dogs – 8 per side
  • Finisher: Plank – hold 1 minute

Day 2 – Upper Body (Weights)

  • Dumbbell shoulder press – 10–12 reps
  • Bent-over rows – 10–12 reps
  • Chest press – 10–12 reps
  • Bicep curls – 12 reps
  • Tricep overhead extensions – 12 reps
  • Side planks – 30 sec each side

Day 3 – Lower Body + Core

  • Goblet squats – 12 reps
  • Dumbbell RDLs – 10 reps
  • Step-ups or lunges – 8–10 reps/leg
  • Calf raises – 20 reps
  • Kettlebell sumo squats – 10 reps
  • Leg raises – 12 reps
  • Russian twists – 20 total

Day 4 – Full Body (Hybrid – KB/DB)

  • Kettlebell swings – 15–20 reps
  • Push-ups – 10–15 reps
  • Dumbbell rows – 10/side
  • Goblet squats – 15 reps
  • Dumbbell/Kettlebell deadlifts – 10 reps
  • Farmer’s carry – 30 seconds
  • Sit-ups or crunches – 15 reps
